Ha Long Bubble Pearl Yogurt

THE START OF A BRAND

Mr. Pham Manh Ha, a native of Ha Long, was born into a family with a tradition of making pearl yogurt since the 1990s. This delicious dish nurtured his childhood and became a fond memory every time. he is far from home. Not only for Mr. Ha, pearl yogurt has crept into every corner of Ha Long city, becoming a familiar dish in the food of the people here. For a long time, Mr. Ha has always dreamed of replicating this dish, not only stopping at the S-shaped strip of land, but also reaching out to the international market to introduce to friends from all over the world the quintessence of his homeland's cuisine. But that dream only truly began when he met two close companions later.
